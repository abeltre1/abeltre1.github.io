Welcome

I am a computer scientist working on high-performance computing and AI infrastructure. My interests span HPC–cloud convergence, containerized systems (Kubernetes/OpenShift, Podman), large-scale LLM inference serving (vLLM), workload scheduling (SLURM, Flux, Kubernetes schedulers), and agentic AI systems built on the Model Context Protocol. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science and have over a decade of experience in cloud and AI systems.

Before my current role, I worked on energy-aware orchestration for hybrid quantum–classical computing and on deploying generative AI services at HPC centers.
